LA ALMAZARA


Vera is a private oil mill, founded by José Vera Miranda, who began its activity in 1951 (What makes it one of the oldest mills in Andalucía), which currently reaches a production level around a million litters per year.


Since its foundation, Vera has focused its efforts on the production of high quality extra virgin olive oil, which is cold-extracted directly from the olives and only by means of mechanical processes, giving rise to an intense and fruity flavour natural olive juice.


This is achieved in very different facilities from the original 1951, which had stone mills and mechanical presses, in a discontinuous extraction process.





The current large and modern facilities were put into operation in the 2016/2017 season. They are located in the northwestern part of the Vega region of Granada (Andalucía, Spain), specifically in Pinos Puente, where the climate is ideal for the growth of the olive tree, being the predominant olive variety of this area the picual.


Extensive experience in this sector, which has been handed down from generation to generation until nowadays, results in an exceptional treatment to customers and suppliers, providing not only the usual services but also other facilities such as transporting the olives from the farmer or pruning collection.
